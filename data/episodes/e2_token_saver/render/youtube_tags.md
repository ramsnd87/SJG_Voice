## WHAT NOT TO TAG

Avoid these — they trigger dilution or category mismatch:

- ❌ Generic single words: `ai`, `coding`, `tools` (drowned in noise)
- ❌ Off-topic-but-trending: `chatgpt`, `gpt-4`, `gemini` (mismatch → CTR drop)
- ❌ Misleading: `free ai tokens` (clickbait flag risk)
- ❌ Repeat brand spam: don't duplicate `token saver studio` 3+ times
